Ideal Job

For me I would really want to work in a job that is chill and does not have a dress code and instead were able to wear whatever we want. However, system administrator would also be a nice job.

The system administrators’ position is responsible for installing, upgrading, and monitoring all the software and hardware. They are also required to diagnose and resolve all IT related problems that occur swiftly.

This position first caught my attention because it is closely related to some of my interests such as handling hardware components and exploring computer software, this is the same for a system administrator where they are responsible for all hardware and software linked issues. Althought this job does seem very intriguing it still has some cons to it, the skills, qualifications, and experience needed are quite demanding. It requires you to be skilful in handling different types of server operating environments, along with managing complex customer environments.

The qualifications needed for this place include Microsoft certifications, Cisco certifications, VMware, and in terms of the experience they must have 10 or more years of experience in the industry.

You also need to be experienced in areas such as:

  • Microsoft cloud services
  • Information security and system hardening
  • Windows server, Linux Operating Systems
  • Virtualisation (Hyper-V, VMWare, Citrix)
  • Tier 1 IT Infrastructure (Network, Storage and Servers)
  • Remote Infrastructure Management Systems
  • Cisco switching and routing
  • General Systems Administration

In terms of my skills, qualifications, and experience, for the experience I only have a simple understanding of networking, using Cisco, and information about general systems administration like Active Directory, DNS, and DHCP. As for skills and qualifications, I have neither of them, but I am aiming to obtain them through internships in the near future.

To meet skills, qualifications, and experience I plan on applying for an internship placement at a decent company with a good reputation if possible, to help me set my foundation in the industry, then enrol in short courses soon to expand my knowledge and obtain some necessary qualifications.
